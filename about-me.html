Know More About Me

SALMAN MITHANI

Musical artist

Salman’s first brush with fame was his first single itself. Once the song did immensely well, he rose as the next big musical star on social media platforms such as YouTube and Instagram. His second single, where he teamed up with another singer, Shahzeb Tejani, was “Apna Bana Le Na.”
This song was also loved by millions. In between his songs, there was a situation when one of his songs was removed from all the platforms, owing to some confusion. However, Salman rose above that setback and promised his fans that he would be back.
Salman has been doing music for many years now and has been backed by Zee Music Company. His career started with Tu Jo Kahe which shined a tremendous amount of spotlight on this new artist.It was with Apna Bana Le Na, he gained a huge amount of following and now with Ishq Da Rog, he has proven his talent in this industry. Salman has been singing prior to his releases and has opened many shows for many Bollywood artists. Salman started his career on YouTube, but always wanted to release on bigger platforms and now he definitely showed what he is capable of.
